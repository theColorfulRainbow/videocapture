PowerPoint:
To insert the newly dowloaded PDF topics slides into your already pre-existing PowerPoint slide simply

1) Extract and then open the Topic PDF you would like to add, in Adobe Acrobat Reader
2) At the top right menu bar under view select fullscreen
 a) If this fullscreen option is un-highlightable (is not available) then make sure you are in edit mode
3) Open up your slides, that you would like to insert the topics into, using PowerPoint
4) Create a new slide and then click insert, located at the top toolbar
5) Click on screenshot
6) Select the Adobe Acrobat Reader as your source of screenshot (image with the topic number)
7) Repeat steps 1-6 until you have successfully added all the PDF's into your topic slides
